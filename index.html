balance

Balance is a window into a young womans life. We follow her as she struggles to find herself and acceptance amidst her life, her memories, and the night.

She is complex. Like a flower she is strong, vibrant but vulnerable. Her life is coloured with a diverse blend of emotions, thoughts and feelings. Balance is not a seemingly straightforward narrative because life, her life, is not straightforward.

Due to the conflicting elements within her narrative, she must find ways to negotiate them and create/find balance.A film by Ellie Berry and Ava Brown-Mantha. Girl played by Anastasia Syzmanski, S.O. played by Sean Balsillie. Score composed by Sean Balsillie, Matt Bettinson and Jocelyn Lee. Poetry by Rupi Kaur.

Watch it at vimeo.com/196976274.
